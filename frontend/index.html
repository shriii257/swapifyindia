How SwapifyIndia Works

Simple, transparent, and fair for both buyers and sellers.

01

List Your Ticket

Upload your unused ticket at or below face value. Add event details and a blurred preview. Listing is completely free.

02

Buyer Pays Securely

A fan finds your ticket, pays the face value via Razorpay. We deduct a 10% platform fee only on successful sales.

03

Buyer gets the full ticket with QR code. Seller gets 90% of the ticket price. If unsold, ticket returns to you 2 hours before the event.
